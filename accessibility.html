Accessibility

We want our website to be usable for everyone, including people with disabilities. Our goal is to meet WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines and keep improving the experience as standards and assistive technologies evolve.

  • Readable contrast and scalable text
  • Keyboard-friendly navigation
  • Meaningful link text and image alt text
  • Clear labels for forms and controls

If you encounter any barriers or need an alternate format (for example, an accessible PDF), please contact us. We’ll make reasonable efforts to accommodate your request.
